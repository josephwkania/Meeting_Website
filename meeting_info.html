Accommodation


For those in need of support for accommodation expenses, please indicate this on the sign up form or contact the LOC. Further information on the subsidised accommodation will be provided here shortly. Some suggestions from the LOC for general hotel options around central Manchester include:

  • Pendulum Hotel
  • Premier Inn - Manchester Central , Manchester City Centre (Portland Street), Manchester City Centre (Princess Street)
  • Motel One, Piccadilly
  • Ibis - Manchester Centre, Manchester Styles,
where some rooms may be set aside for attendees of the Meeting (any updates on this will be communicated here in due course). A comprehensive list of further accommodation options can be found on Visit Manchester, or on Booking.com.

Venue


The Meeting will take place in the Schuster Building at the University of Manchester, Manchester M13 9PL. We will use the Rutherford Lecture Theatre, Jocelyn Bell Burnell Lecture Theatre, and Schuster Foyer. There will also be smaller meeting rooms available for personal use and free discussion.
Manchester is linked by rail to a range of major cities across the UK, and is served by Manchester Airport. General travel guidance provided by the University of Manchester can be found here
